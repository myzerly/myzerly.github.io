Equinix: WebApp Dashboard

For Equinix, there was a set of criteria that we couldn't go outside of. They wanted to keep the site's width at 960px, the color palette needed to be the same greys and reds and we needed to preserve the menu as it was currently displayed. This darker view won them over with the direction of the icons that highlighted the status of the data center. All icons were made in Illustrator, but the comp was put together in Photoshop
