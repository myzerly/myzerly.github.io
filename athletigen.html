Athletigen: iOS App Visual Direction

Athletigen was looking for a couple of different ways to visualize data that they derived from DNA tests through an iOS application. We named this visual direction "Shotput" because it's a darker edgier look than the others that were lighter and used much more white space. The emphasis in this visual direction was the use of "bubbles" to visualize the hierarchy of "gifts" and "opportunities". These were made using Sketch 3 and the custom items were made using Illustrator.
