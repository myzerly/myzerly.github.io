About Me

Hey! My name is Nic Edwards and I live in Santa Cruz, California. I love climbing, trail running, lifting and just walking around downtown sipping on some Verve coffee. In a prior life I used to cook and worked with Bruno Chemel of Baumé, a two Michelin starred restaurant. I graduated from UC Davis in 2011 with a degree in East Asian Studies with an emphasis on Japanese language and culture.

By day I’m a Product Designer with experience working with engineers, front-end devs and customers. I worked on Notable for close to a year and a half while at ZURB Inc., a design consultancy that services companies such as Mozilla, Netflix, Samsung and Disney.


About this Site

This site is hand coded using Foundation for the grid and the box grid. It uses HTML, SASS, JQuery and some Angular as well. The custom icons were created using Illustrator and the visual assets were a mish-mash between PS, AI and Sketch. Photos are courtesy of the iPhone 6.
